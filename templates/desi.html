What is speech therapy?

Speech therapy is treatment that improves your ability to talk and use other language skills. It helps you express your thoughts and understand what other people are saying to you. It can also improve skills like your memory and ability to solve problems.

What is speech disorder?

Speech sound disorders are an umbrella term referring to any difficulty or combination of difficulties with perception, motor production, or phonological representation of speech sounds and speech segments including phonotactic rules governing permissible speech sound sequences in a language.

What are its causes?

Often, a speech sound disorder has no known cause. But some speech sound errors may be caused by: Brain injury. Thinking or development disability. Problems with hearing or hearing loss, such as past ear infections.

Statistical analysis

Around 11 to 12 million people are estimated to stutter in India. Stuttering is more common in children than in adults, with 5% of cases occurring in children compared to only 1% in the adult population. The main causes of stuttering can be stress and pressure.
